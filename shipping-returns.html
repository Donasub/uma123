Shipping, returns, and a few honest promises.

"We ship from Ikeja. We don't pretend to ship same-day from Mars. Here's what actually happens after you hit buy."

All orders are picked, packed, and dispatched from our Lagos print facility. Delivery times below are from dispatch, not from order. Custom/made-to-order pieces add 2–3 production days — noted at checkout.

Returns & exchanges

14 days, no drama.

Didn't fit right? Wrong colour in person? Changed your mind? Send it back within 14 days of delivery — we'll refund to original payment or swap it for the right size.

Returns are free for all Nigerian orders. Zone 3–5 customers cover return shipping; we cover the rest.

01

Start your return

Reply to your order confirmation email or message us on WhatsApp. We'll send a prepaid label within 2 hours (business hours).

02

Pack it properly

Original condition — unworn, unwashed, tags attached. Reuse our box if you have it. If not, any clean packaging works.

03

Drop & track

Drop at any Redstar Express pickup point or book a pickup. You'll get a tracking link to follow it back to us.

04

Refund in 3–5 days

Once we receive it, QA takes 24 hours, refund lands in 3–5 working days. Swaps ship same day if your new size is in stock.

The fine print, in plain English

What we can and can't take back.

Returnable

We'll take it back if:

  • It's within 14 days of delivery
  • Unworn, unwashed, original tags attached
  • Original condition — no deodorant or perfume marks
  • Packaging is intact where possible
  • You have the order number or email
Non-returnable

We can't take back:

  • Custom-printed or personalised pieces (names, numbers)
  • Gift cards — once activated
  • Final sale items (marked clearly at checkout)
  • Underwear, socks, or intimate items
  • Anything that's been worn, washed, or altered
