The Four Steps

From pick to parcel.

Four clean steps between you and merch that actually reps your campus. No fakes, no knockoffs, no waiting weeks for something shipped from abroad.

01
Step 01

Pick your campus.

Choose from our partner network of 20 universities and 20 high schools across Nigeria. Each school has its own curated storefront — your crest, your colors, your abbreviation on every product.

Unis · High schools · Boarding · Day
02
Step 02

Browse the drop.

Hoodies, tees, caps, varsity jackets, tote bags — all designed in-house around your school's identity. Filter by category, size, or color. Every item officially licensed through the school.

5 categories · Sizes XS to XXL
03
Step 03

Pick size & color.

Open any product to choose your size and preferred colorway. We offer at least 3 colorways per item, anchored by your school's primary color. Add to bag, keep browsing, or check out straight away.

Step 04

Choose campus pickup (free), doorstep delivery, or express — and pay by card, bank transfer, or USSD. We'll send tracking by email and SMS the moment your parcel leaves our Lagos warehouse.
