How we work

Six things we actually mean.

Every company has values. Most are posters. Ours are operating principles — the ones we lean on when a decision is hard, a drop is late, or a partnership gets messy.

If these resonate, you'll probably enjoy working here. If they make you roll your eyes, you probably won't — and that's fine too.

Craft over churn

We'd rather drop one excellent hoodie a month than ten mediocre ones a week.

Show the receipts

Transparency with partners, with customers, with each other. If you can't explain the decision in a pager message, it's not the right decision.

Respect the crest

We treat every licensed mark like it's our family's name. Because to someone, it is.

Lagos first, Africa next

We build for the continent, not for export. Our defaults are Naira, WAT, tropical weight fabric.

Ship the small wins

Small improvements, shipped weekly, compound faster than big launches shipped quarterly.

Play the long alumni game

We want customers for 40 years, not 40 days. That shapes every decision we make.

The deal

What we offer.

01

Above-market pay

Benchmarked quarterly against Lagos tech & design salaries. Transparent bands, no haggling games.

02

Equity from day one

Every full-time hire gets stock options. We want you to own a piece of what you build.

03

Full health cover

Private HMO for you and your immediate family, dental included. No waiting period.

04

Real time off

25 days paid leave + public holidays. We actually enforce it — your manager will nag you if you don't take it.

05

Learning budget

₦400k/year for courses, conferences, or books. No pre-approval required under ₦150k.

06

All the merch

Every drop, on us. Plus a ₦50k annual merch stipend for friends and family. It's the least we can do.
