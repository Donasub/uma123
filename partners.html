The institutions we build with.

"A licensing partner isn't just a logo agreement. It's a promise to treat your crest like we'd treat our own."

We work directly with university administrations, student affairs offices, and alumni associations across Africa. Every sale returns a royalty to the institution — funding scholarships, facilities, and student programmes. This page is where we celebrate them.

Our approach

Licensing done properly.

When we say "officially licensed," we mean signed agreements with your VC's office. Approved artwork. Audited royalty statements, delivered quarterly. A proper paper trail — because that's what protects your institution.

Every partner gets a dedicated account manager, a co-branded landing page, pre-launch review on every product, and full transparency into what's selling, to whom, and where.

"They're the first company that actually showed up with a proper MOU, a royalty model, and a team that understood what a crest means to an institution like ours."

— Deputy Registrar, partner university

How it works

From conversation to crest on a hoodie.

A typical partnership moves from first call to first drop in about 6–8 weeks.

01

First conversation

A call with your student affairs or alumni office. No pitch deck — just a proper chat about what your institution needs.

02

MOU & royalty model

We draft a licensing agreement with your legal team, agreed royalty rate, and pre-launch approval clause. Nothing moves without signatures.

03

Design & sampling

Our design team works with your brand guidelines. You review physical samples before anything ships or sells.

04

Launch & royalties

Your co-branded storefront goes live. Quarterly royalty statements + audit reports, on the same schedule every time.
