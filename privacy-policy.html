Privacy Policy.

"Plain language where we can, legal language where we must. Your data, how we handle it, and how you take it back."

This policy explains what personal information we collect when you use universemerch.africa, what we do with it, who we share it with, and the rights you have over it — under the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023, the NDPR, and GDPR where applicable.

§01

Who we are.

Universe Merch Africa Limited ("Universe Merch," "we," "us," "our") is a private company incorporated in the Federal Republic of Nigeria, registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission. Our registered address is Plot 14, Industrial Avenue, Ilupeju Bypass, Ikeja, Lagos 100252.

We operate universemerch.africa (the "Site") and the associated mobile and web applications. When we talk about "you" in this policy, we mean anyone who visits the Site, creates an account, or buys something from us.

For the purposes of data protection law, we are the Data Controller of the personal information collected through the Site.

§02

What we collect.

We try to collect only what we actually need to run the business. Specifically:

Information you give us directly

  • Name, email, phone number, delivery address
  • Password (we never see it in plain text — it's hashed)
  • University or school affiliation (optional, for campus pickup)
  • Order history, size preferences, wishlist items
  • Messages you send us via contact forms or WhatsApp

Information collected automatically

  • IP address, approximate location (city-level)
  • Device type, browser, operating system
  • Pages you visit, time on site, referring URL
  • Cookies and similar tracking (see Section 5)

Information from third parties

  • Payment processors confirming successful transactions
  • Shipping carriers providing delivery status updates
  • Authentication providers (if you sign in with Google, etc.)
§03

How we use it.

Under Nigerian law, we need a lawful basis to process your data. For us, that means:

  • To fulfil your order (contract) — sending your purchase to the right address, handling returns, providing customer support.
  • To run our business (legitimate interest) — preventing fraud, improving the Site, analysing what products sell.
  • To market to you (consent) — sending drop alerts, restock notifications, and offers you've signed up for. You can opt out at any time.
  • To meet legal obligations — tax records, consumer protection requirements, responding to lawful requests.
What we don't do: we don't sell your personal information. We don't share it with advertisers. We don't use it to train AI models. Full stop.
§04

Who we share with.

We share personal information only with the companies that help us run the business. Each of them has signed a data-processing agreement with us. They are:

  • Payment processors — Paystack, Flutterwave, Stripe (for international orders)
  • Shipping carriers — DHL, Redstar Express, FedEx, local couriers
  • Communication tools — email delivery, SMS gateway, WhatsApp Business
  • Analytics — privacy-respecting, aggregated only (no Google Analytics)
  • Licensed partner institutions — only aggregate sales data (not individual buyers) for royalty reporting

We may also share information if required by a Nigerian court order, law enforcement request, or to protect our rights — but only to the extent legally required.

§05

Cookies & tracking.

We use a small number of cookies. You can see them all, and disable non-essential ones, in your browser or via the cookie banner on your first visit.

  • Essential — keeps you logged in, remembers your cart, prevents fraud. Can't be turned off.
  • Functional — remembers your preferences like currency and language.
  • Analytics — counts visitors and measures page performance in an aggregated way. We do not use tools that profile you individually.

We do not use cross-site tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or retargeting tags. If you've seen a Universe Merch ad following you around the internet, it wasn't from us.

§06

Payments & fraud.

We do not store your full card number, CVV, or bank credentials on our servers — ever. Payment details are handled directly by PCI-DSS compliant processors (Paystack, Flutterwave, Stripe) and we only receive a tokenised confirmation that the payment succeeded.

For fraud prevention, we retain information about the device and network used for an order for up to 24 months. This helps us stop chargeback fraud without holding your payment data longer than needed.

§07

How long we keep it.

We keep personal information only for as long as we need it for the purpose it was collected:

  • Account data — while your account is active, plus 12 months after you delete it
  • Order records — 7 years (to meet Nigerian tax and consumer protection law)
  • Marketing preferences — until you opt out or your account is deleted
  • Support conversations — 24 months
  • Anonymised analytics — indefinitely (but no longer linked to you)
§08

Your rights.

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 and, where applicable, GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — ask for a copy of your personal data
  • Correct — fix anything we have wrong
  • Delete — ask us to erase your data (subject to legal retention)
  • Object — to certain types of processing, including direct marketing
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Withdraw consent — at any time, without penalty
  • Complain — to the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC)

To exercise any of these, email our DPO at dpo@universemerch.africa. We'll respond within 30 days, as required by law.

§09

How we protect it.

We take security seriously. This means:

  • All traffic to and from the Site is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3)
  • Stored data is encrypted at rest using industry-standard algorithms
  • Passwords are hashed with bcrypt — never stored in readable form
  • Access to customer data is restricted to employees who need it, logged, and reviewed monthly
  • We run regular penetration tests and have a responsible-disclosure programme

If a breach ever affects your personal data, we'll notify you and the NDPC within 72 hours, as required by law.

§10

Children & minors.

The Site is not intended for children under 13, and we don't knowingly collect personal information from them. For our high-school merchandise, we assume orders are placed by alumni, staff, or parents/guardians — not students under 18 acting alone.

If you believe a child has submitted personal information to us, contact our DPO and we'll delete it immediately.

§11

Changes to this policy.

We'll update this policy when our practices change or when the law requires. When we make a material change (affecting how we use your data), we'll email everyone with an active account at least 14 days before it takes effect.

Minor changes (like updating an address or clarifying wording) will just be posted here. The version number and date at the top of this page always reflect the current version. Previous versions are available on request.

§12

Contact our DPO.

Every question, complaint, or request relating to your data should go to our Data Protection Officer. They're based in our Lagos office and acknowledge all requests within 48 hours.
